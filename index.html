🚀 Getting Started
New users see a 3-slide tour on first login, followed by a choice: Quick Start (3 pre-built activities + 2 challenges created for you) or Build My Own. You can always edit or delete anything afterwards. Your progress auto-saves to the cloud every time you complete an activity.
🗂️ Dimensions → Paths → Activities
Go to Activities → Categories to build your structure. Create a Dimension (a big life area, e.g. Health), add a Path inside it (e.g. Exercise), then add Activities to each path (e.g. Morning Run). This hierarchy keeps everything organised and powers the spider chart in your Profile.
✅ Completing Activities & Earning XP
The My Activities sub-tab shows what's due today. Tap any card to mark it done and earn XP — tap again the same day to undo. Activity slots unlock as you level up: L1 = 4, L2 = 5, L3 = 7, L4 = 11, growing quickly. Negative activities drain XP when completed (for habits you want to avoid) and Skip-negative activities penalise you on missed days.
📅 Frequency Types
Daily — resets every day, builds streaks. Occasional — available any day, no streak pressure; optionally deletes itself after completion. Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly — periodic windows, build streaks. Custom — you set the interval in days or choose specific weekdays. Occasional activities show an "occasional" badge in the Daily Planner so you can schedule them too.
🗓️ Daily Planner
Go to Activities → Daily Planner to schedule activities at specific times. Tap + Add to pick any activity (including occasional ones) or add a free-text note. Toggle Recurring to repeat the item every day. Use the date arrows to plan ahead or review past days. Completing an item in the planner awards XP just like completing it from the main list.
🔥 Streaks, Shields & Bonus XP
Completing recurring activities consistently builds your streak. From streak 5 onward you earn exponential bonus XP — streak 10 = 4×, streak 20 = 9×, streak 30 = 17× base XP. Streak Shields (up to 3 per activity) absorb missed days automatically so one off-day doesn't reset your streak. Shields regenerate over time. Occasional activities don't track streaks.
⬆️ Levelling & Dimensions
Your global level uses the formula round(k × (2L − 1)) where k defaults to 8.5 — adjust it in Level Scaling. Each Dimension also has its own level that tracks XP earned inside it specifically. Dimension levels unlock cosmetic rewards you can assign in your Profile. Negative activities can drain XP within a level but can never de-level you.
🏆 Challenges
Personal Challenges are time-boxed goals that award bonus XP on completion — link specific activities and set target counts for each. Group Challenges (Friends tab) let you create a shared challenge where each member nominates their own personal challenge to contribute. The group leaderboard tracks everyone's momentum. Completed challenges can be retaken with "Take Again".
👥 Friends & Social
Go to the Friends tab to add friends by their MK-XXXX friend code (tap your avatar → Profile to find yours). You can view a friend's level, spider chart and top activities on their profile card. Share your progress with the Share Card when you level up — a beautiful image you can post anywhere.
🕸️ Spider Chart & Life Balance
Your Profile shows a spider chart mapping your XP across five life categories: Body, Mind, People, Work, and Extra. Go to Profile → Configure Chart to assign each activity to a category. The chart updates live and is also visible on your friends' profile cards.
📊 Analytics
The Analytics tab shows your XP over time as a line chart. Use the series picker to overlay any combination of dimensions, paths, or individual activities. Switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views. The Activity History section (below the chart) shows a full log — including entries from activities you've since deleted, which are marked with a † symbol.
🎨 Custom Themes
Scroll to Theme in Settings to pick a preset or build a fully custom look. In Custom mode, adjust every colour variable, background glows, and gradient direction. Save up to 3 named template slots — clicking a saved slot applies it instantly and auto-saves. Hit Apply Theme to persist any manual edits.
☁️ Backup & Data
Mindkraft auto-creates a daily cloud backup every time you save data. Hit Backup Now to force an immediate snapshot. Use Restore Backup to roll back to the last saved snapshot — this fetches the latest version directly from the cloud. You can also Export a full JSON file at any time and Import it on any device.
🎁 Rewards & Profile
Open your Profile (tap your avatar) to set a username, view your stats, and define Rewards — real-world treats tied to level milestones (e.g. "Buy new headphones at Level 10"). Rewards also unlock on Dimension levels. A special Level 100 surprise awaits at the top. Your character title evolves based on which life category you invest in most.
