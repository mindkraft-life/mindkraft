🗺️ Step 1 — Build your Structure
Go to Activities → Categories and create a life area (e.g. Health). Add a Path inside it (e.g. Fitness). Then add Activities to each path (e.g. Morning Run). This hierarchy keeps everything organised.
📋 Step 2 — Complete Activities & Earn XP
The Activities tab shows what's due. Tap to mark done and earn XP. Tap again to undo (same day only). Slots: L1 = 4 slots, L2 = 5, L3 = 7, L4 = 11 — growing fast as you level up.
📅 Frequency Types
Daily — resets every day, builds streaks. Occasional — available every day but no streak pressure. Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly — periodic, builds streaks. Custom — you choose the interval in days.
🔥 Streaks & Bonus XP
Completing recurring activities consistently grows your streak. From streak 5 onward, you earn an exponential bonus: streak 10 = 4×, streak 20 = 9×, streak 30 = 17× base XP. Occasional activities don't track streaks.
⬆️ Leveling
XP per level scales linearly: round(8.5 × (2L − 1)). L1→2 costs 9 XP, rising smoothly — L10 needs 162 XP, L50 needs 842 XP. Negative activities drain XP within your current level but can't de-level you. Undoing an activity restores the XP and level correctly.
🏆 Challenges & 🎁 Rewards
Challenges are time-boxed goals for bonus XP — link specific activities and set how many times each must be done. Rewards (in your Profile) attach real-world treats to level milestones. A Level 100 surprise awaits!
